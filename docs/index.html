HAMON

A universal, lossless harmony-label interlingua — one typed model for chord symbols, Roman numerals, Nashville numbers, figured bass and functional analysis, round-tripped across MEI · MusicXML · Humdrum · LilyPond · ABC · MuseScore · Harte · iReal Pro · DCML · RomanText · Dezrann.

Representative examples exported to every encoding, an interactive xencoding loss viewer, and the poster figures.
